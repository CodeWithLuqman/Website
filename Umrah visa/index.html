Umrah Visa

An Umrah visa is a travel document issued by the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA), allowing you to enter the country to perform the Umrah pilgrimage. You can complete this religious journey anytime during the year.

Umrah Visa Processing Time

In normal circumstances, an Umrah visa takes two to three days to finish processing — depending on your country of residence.

Umrah Visa Validity

The Umrah visa is valid for two weeks. But, if you are performing Umrah during Ramadan, you must leave by the last day of the holy month regardless of whether you have stayed two weeks or not.

Visa Documents Required

  • Passport Scan Copy
  • 1 Passport Size Photo (Not older than 6 months, with white background)
  • CNIC Copy
