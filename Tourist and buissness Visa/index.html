Tourist/Business Visa

A tourist visa is an official document or stamp that authorizes an individual to enter a foreign country for the purpose of leisure and tourism.

A business visa is a travel authorization that allows every applicant to visit a foreign country temporarily for business purposes like Conference Meetings, Business Meetings & Exhibitions

Tourist/Business Visa Processing Time

In normal circumstances, a Tourist/Business Visa takes two to three weeks to finish processing (some applications take more time for decision).

Tourist/Business Visa Validity

Tourist/Business Visa is valid for three months.
